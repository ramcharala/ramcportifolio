Playbook

Principles, frameworks, and checklists I use repeatedly

← Back

Strategy

  • Define product strategy with a clear vision, target, and guardrails. Keep it front & center through quarterly reviews.
  • Use outcome‑based roadmaps (Now/Next/Later) tied to OKRs.
  • State explicit non‑goals to prevent scope creep.

Discovery & Delivery

  • Frame problems via JTBD & service blueprints; validate with thin slices.
  • Write lean PRDs emphasizing metrics, constraints, and risks.
  • Establish definition of ready/done and acceptance criteria (BDD).

Integrations & Quality

  • Treat APIs as first‑class products: versioning, changelogs, deprecation policy.
  • Adopt consumer‑driven contracts and test in CI.
  • Monitor SLIs/SLOs; wire alerts to ownership; publish runbooks.

Operations & Change

  • Plan wave deployments with readiness checklists and rollback paths.
  • Measure adoption with funnel instrumentation & feedback loops.
  • Close the loop with training, office hours, and self‑service docs.
